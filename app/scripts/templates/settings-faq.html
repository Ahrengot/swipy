FAQ

  • How often do you synchronise the data?

    All content changes are synced constantly.

  • Can I quickly assign tags to a task?

    While adding a task, write the tag you want to assign it with a hashtag sign (#). Eg. Buy new ink cartridge #shopping #office

  • How do I snooze a task for a specific time?

    To snooze a task for a specific time, you need to swipe it to the left and access the scheduler. Then you choose the “Pick a Date” option, you select a date and move the slider below it to adjust the time.

  • How can I use the web app on my iPad?

    Open the web app on the browser of your iPad, log in and you’re ready to go. To have a quick access to it, you can create an icon on the home screen. Here’s a short video on how you can do that: Link to video

  • How can I manage my Plus subscription?

    You can change your subscriptions from the "Manage" button in the Settings of your Swipes iPhone app. This is an auto-renewable subscription in accordance to App Store rules. Subscriptions will be charged to your credit card through your iTunes account. Your subscription will automatically renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period.

    Please, notice: If you terminate or downgrade a Swipes Plus Account, the original agreements will run to the end of the agreed term and will then no longer be extended. Reimbursement of already-paid fees for the current term is not possible.

  • Why cannot I use shortcuts on the keyboard to manage tasks?

    At the moment shortcuts are not available for the web app. We will introduce them in the updates updates to make your experience faster and smoother.

  • How do I delete my account?

    If you want to delete your account, please inform us at support@swipesapp.com. We will also delete all data associated to your account. We’ll be grateful if you add the reasons for leaving us, so we can constantly improve the service.
